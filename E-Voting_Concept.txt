----Process
Step 1 - Election setup
Authority :
	- generate Paillier keypair
	- publish public key
	- keep private kety secret

Step 2 - Voter Authentication
User:
	- login
	- verify ID
	- OTP/email
--> Only vote 1 time


Step 3 - Encrypt Vote
ex:
Candidate A -> 1
Candidate B ->2


Step 4 - Sign Ballot

Vote use:
	- private signing key

--> signature = Sign(ciphertext)

	--> Against:
		- fake ballot
		- modified ballot

Step 5 - Submit to Blockchain

Smart contract save:
	- ciphertext
	- signature
	- timestamp	
	- vote receipt

Step 6 - Bulletin Board
Public can:
	- verify vote exists
	- audit election

But:
	- no one know who was voted

Step 7 - Homomorphic tally
Server take ciphertext from blockchain --> calculate formula

Step 8 - Final Decryption
Authority decode
	--> Total ballot
